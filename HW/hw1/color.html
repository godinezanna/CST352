Emphasis Using Colors

Color is the element of art that is made up of three properties:

  • Hue: The name of the color
  • Value: The darkness or lightness of the hue (a colors value will change when you add black or white)
  • Intensity: The quality of purity or brightness (high intensity = strong and bright; low intensity = faint and dull)
